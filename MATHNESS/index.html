About Us

i-Learning Math is the leading ICT-based educational product in Malaysia and it has helped thousands and thousands of students to excel in the national SPM examinations. The product was first launched back in 1997 as an output learning programme.

Our website is a Mathematics tutorial and reference website for SPM students. With our up-to-date Maths solutions from time to time, student is able to download and make it as a reference. i-Learning Math also provides video lectures, exercises and solutions, and Maths formulas. Our team also are working hard in recording online tutorial session to make it as interesting as it is. This to cater the injustice perception of students thinking that Mathematics is boring and hard to score. Our purpose also is to let student discovered their self learning skills at the same time explore the technologies while learning.
